10,000 Roses

10,000 Roses is good for tourist spot specially during at night. You can see the beauty of the roses during at night, you can also find a coffee shop which you can enjoy watching the view while sipping a nice hot or cold coffee.

Parola

At Parola Seaview Restaurant you can enjoy the authentic Filipino food while watching the view and sunrise or sunset, they also serve some good and delicious beverages.

Bantayan

Bantayan is a great spot for swimming, you can bring your friends and family and enjoy the view while swimming.

Entoy's Bakasihan

In Entoy's Bakasihan you can eat authentic dishes while watching the sunrise or sunset.
